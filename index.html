The Vision

Why ShrinkWatt Exists

!!! For residential Con Edison customers who have no year-round visibility into what drives their energy bill or how to reduce it, ShrinkWatt is a web application that uses AI-generated, personalized insights and gamified rewards to motivate consistent energy savings. Unlike Con Edison's existing peak-event programs, which only engage customers during a handful of summer demand-response events, ShrinkWatt delivers year-round, explainable, individualized guidance and rewards — independent of the utility's own event schedule.
